Introducing Politics Genie - Your one stop digital politics platform.

Our digital politics platform provides all capabilities to help politicians carry out their duties in an efficient manner. It helps them keep track of their political history, engage with their voter base, understand the pulse of the people and much more.


The 4 key pillars for the success of any political party are:
  1. Understanding the needs of the people
  2. Timely action
  3. Transparency
  4. Trust


1.Understanding the needs of the people


By establishing a dedicated communication channel with the public through our digital solutions like surveys and grievance cell the politicians can understand the pain points of the public and take appropriate actions.


2.Timely action


The public can raise their concerns via our digital platform, and this goes to the attention of their politician immediately.This enables the politician to act on pressing issues immediately with might otherwise not be the case due to the involvement of multiple middlemen/laborious manual processes.


3.Transparency


Transparency is core foundation of our platform.At any point in time the public can track the status of their requests through our digital platform. The politicians also have a transparent track of their administrative tasks which can be used as solid points for future election campaigns. The platform establishes proper ownership and governance.


4.Trust


Trust is the basis of all relationships be it personal or political.By leveraging the capabilities of our digital platform, a mutual trust can be established between the public and the political party / politician. By adopting a data driven approach that is tamper-proof long-lasting ties are made between both parties.

  • Grievance Management to help solve people issues in a timely manner
  • Campaign Management for efficient voter base coverage
  • Insights
  • One click solutions
